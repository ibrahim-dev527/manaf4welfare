Three-Point Welfare Plan

The Plan for
Student Wellbeing.

01
Mental Health
Mental Health Struggles & Counseling

Students face mounting mental health pressures with little access to qualified support. Isolation, stress, and anxiety are student realities that cannot be ignored.

Establish a peer-support network of trained student counselors available across campus.
02
Campus Facilities
Poor Washrooms & Accommodation

Substandard washroom conditions and limited housing options rob students of the dignity and comfort they deserve as members of this institution.

Launch a maintenance feedback system and conduct structured campus facility audits.
03
Financial Support
Limited Financial Resources & Aid

Students in financial hardship have no safety net. Scholarships go unclaimed, emergency funds don't exist, and students suffer in silence.

Set up an emergency welfare fund and provide active scholarship & financial aid information.
